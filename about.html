About Dr. Yvonne

CEO & Founder

Dr. Yvonne Smith-Canegan

Dr. Yvonne Smith-Canegan is the founder and CEO of Canegan Associates Consulting, a luxury lifestyle reconstruction coaching practice located in Skokie, Illinois. With a rare combination of academic excellence, spiritual wisdom, and lived experience, she has devoted her career to helping clients fully unlock their potential.

Her academic journey includes degrees in Marketing from Columbia Chicago, Legal studies from the American Law Institute, a Masters in Human Resources from NEIU, and a Ph.D. in Business Ethics from the American College of Metaphysical Theology. She is also an Ordained Minister.

Drawing from these diverse disciplines, Dr. Yvonne guides clients through deeply personalized transformations — helping them release limiting beliefs, establish effective gatekeeping strategies, and reconnect with their most authentic, powerful selves.

As a client of her own healing regimens, she brings not only knowledge but genuine lived experience to every coaching relationship. Her approach weaves together spiritual coaching, holistic wellness, and executive-level business strategy into a seamless framework for lasting change.
